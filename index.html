Benefits

Impact Investing

For-profit investors now have a financial vehicle for making money while still having a social impact footprint.

Philanthropy

Philanthropist now have a way to measure the impact of their donations and analyze potential future investments.

Impact Entrepreneurs

Non-profits now have a way to measure their impact, attract investors, find and reward volunteers.

Contributors

Donors and volunteers will be able to see the impact they have on the world and be rewarded for it through a valuable currency.

The millennial generation of investors and entrepreneurs have a new view of how to impact the world.

 
 

Our mission is to create an economy where impact investors, philanthropists, entrepreneurs, and contributors all benefit.

  • Discover More

For too long there has been a conflict between For-Profit and Non-Profit interests through impact investments. The back-and-forth of helping people vs. the need to make money for investors has created a rift in our ability to have sustainable impact on the world.

Impact Reward Tokens address this issue by creating a recipricle economy between investors, entrepreneurs, donors and volunteers. Investors can make money while entrepreneurs focus on doing good. Donors and volunteers have transparency on their impact and are rewarded for it.
